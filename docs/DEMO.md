# Hackathon Demo Flow

## What Judges Should Remember

- The problem happens in the middle of a sentence, not after it.
- Cookie fixes the slip. Cream keeps the conversation alive.
- Nova 2 Sonic powers the live voice loop.
- The project is about recovery inside a real conversation, not generic language tutoring.

## 0:00 - 0:15 Opening Shot

Open on the main screen with the Cream panel on the left and the chat on the right.

Say:
- `Most language tools correct you after you stop talking. Cookie & Cream helps you recover before the conversation dies.`
- `Cream keeps the conversation going in Spanish. Cookie only appears when I slip into English and need a quick fix.`

## 0:15 - 0:30 Explain The Live Loop Honestly

Say:
- `This live voice loop runs on Amazon Nova 2 Sonic.`
- `I speak, the app shows the live utterance right away, Cookie catches English slips, and then Cream keeps the conversation moving.`

## 0:30 - 0:40 Pick A Starter

Click `Cafe order`.

Say:
- `The starter gets me into a realistic scenario quickly so I can show the recovery loop right away.`

## 0:40 - 1:05 Normal Spanish Turn

Tap the mic and say:
- `Quiero pedir un cafe con leche.`

Pause, then let the turn submit.

Say:
- `When I stay in Spanish, Cream responds like a natural conversation partner.`
- `The app shows the spoken turn live, then the reply comes back with audio.`

## 1:05 - 1:35 Show The English Slip

Tap the mic and say:
- `Quiero un latte and a muffin.`

Pause on the whisper state.

Say:
- `Now I slipped into English in the middle of the sentence.`
- `Cookie does not turn this into a lecture. Cookie just gives me the smallest repair that gets me back into Spanish.`

## 1:35 - 1:55 Show The Retry

Tap the mic again and say:
- `Quiero un cafe con leche y un muffin.`

Pause, then let the turn submit.

Say:
- `Once I retry in Spanish, Cream picks the conversation right back up.`

## 1:55 - 2:20 One More Normal Turn

Tap the mic and say:
- `Tambien quiero sentarme cerca de la ventana.`

Say:
- `That is the whole point: recover inside the conversation instead of freezing or restarting.`

## 2:20 - 2:40 Session Recap

Click `Session recap`.

Say:
- `At the end, the app gives a short recap of what went well and what to try next.`

## 2:40 - 2:55 Translation Support

Translate the latest Cream message.

Say:
- `If I need help, I can translate a Cream message on demand without breaking the main live loop.`

## 2:55 - 3:00 Closing Line

Say:
- `The new idea here is the whisper recovery loop: live voice, quick repair, then straight back to conversation.`
- `Nova Sonic powers the live voice loop, and the repo still keeps the earlier reviewed flow as a fallback path.`
