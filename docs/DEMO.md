# Hackathon Demo Flow

## 0:00 - 0:15 Opening Shot

Open on the main screen with the Cream panel on the left and the chat on the right.

Say:
- `Cookie & Cream is a voice-first Spanish conversation coach for English-speaking learners.`
- `Cream is the conversation partner. Cookie is the tutor who appears only when I slip into English.`

## 0:15 - 0:30 Explain The Current Mode Honestly

Say:
- `This version uses a reviewed transcript flow. I can speak or type, edit the transcript, and then send the turn.`
- `The backend text path uses Nova 2 Lite today.`
- `This is not live Sonic streaming mode yet.`

## 0:30 - 0:40 Pick A Starter

Click `Cafe order`.

Say:
- `The scenario starters make it easy to begin a realistic conversation quickly.`

## 0:40 - 1:00 Normal Spanish Turn

Send the prefilled starter:
- `Estoy en una cafetería y quiero pedir un café con leche.`

Say:
- `When I stay in Spanish, Cream keeps the conversation moving naturally in Spanish.`

Pause on Cream’s response.

## 1:00 - 1:20 Show The English Slip

Type or say:
- `Quiero un latte and a muffin.`

Press `Send`.

Say:
- `Now I slipped into English, so the turn should go to Cookie instead of Cream.`

Pause on Cookie’s response.

## 1:20 - 1:40 Cookie Coaching

Say:
- `Cookie stays short, coaches in English, gives one better Spanish phrasing, and asks me to try again.`

## 1:40 - 2:00 Retry In Spanish

Type or say:
- `Quiero un café con leche y un muffin.`

Press `Send`.

Say:
- `After the retry is fully in Spanish, Cream takes over again and continues the conversation.`

Pause on Cream’s next response.

## 2:00 - 2:20 One More Normal Turn

Type or say:
- `También quiero sentarme cerca de la ventana.`

Press `Send`.

Say:
- `The conversation keeps going normally once I recover back into Spanish.`

## 2:20 - 2:40 Session Recap

Click `Session recap`.

Say:
- `At the end, the app can generate a short recap from the current in-memory conversation.`
- `It gives me three things I did well, one better way to say something, and one thing to try next.`

Pause on the recap card.

## 2:40 - 3:00 Closing Line

Say:
- `So the current product already shows the full learning loop: Spanish conversation, an English slip, tutor intervention, Spanish retry, continued conversation, and a short recap.`
- `Today that loop runs through a reviewed transcript flow with Nova 2 Lite on the backend. Live Sonic voice mode is future work.`
